How We Work
Full-Cycle Trading Solutions

We operate across the entire metal recycling and sourcing value chain, providing tailored solutions for producers, traders, and processors.

01

Sourcing & Procurement

Leveraging an extensive supplier network across Europe and beyond to source primary and secondary materials matching exact buyer specifications.

02

Grading & Inspection

Thorough material inspection and content analysis for all traded commodities. Detailed reporting ensures quality, purity, and contractual compliance.

03

Import & Export

Active involvement in cross-border trading. We handle customs documentation, export licenses, and international logistics for efficient redistribution.

04

Basel & TFS Compliance

End-to-end management of notifications and consents for transboundary movement of waste batteries, RDF, and regulated streams under the Basel Convention.

05

Environmental Compliance

Coordination with competent authorities to ensure all collection, transport, and recovery activities comply with EU and national environmental law.

06

Consulting & Advisory

Expert guidance on material classification, regulatory obligations, and optimising the commercial value of waste streams and by-products.
